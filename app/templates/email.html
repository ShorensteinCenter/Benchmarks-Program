User Unique Open Rate is the number of unique emails opened divided by number of total emails received · Subscribed are current subscribers · Unsubscribed are subscribers who removed themselves from list or the list owner removed · Pending are semi-subscribers stuck in the limbo of double opt in · Cleaned are subscribers removed from the list, typically by a service provider after email bounces · Opened in last 365 Days refers to a subscriber whose list activity includes at least one "open" event in the 365 days prior to this analysis. Note that we cache some data to reduce analysis time, so very recent list activity (e.g. occurring in the past 1-2 weeks) may not be reflected in this statistic · Database Average is the mean figure across all lists in our data set.
